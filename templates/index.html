What is Central Experience?

A professional development resource database that connects members of the Wellesley community to opportunities.

Why are we important?

Our focus on accessibility and inclusivity.
Because existing opportunities are decentralized across platforms such as Facebook, LinkedIn, the Hive, G Suite, and more, even before they endure the application and interview process, students must do extensive research just to seek opportunities. Furthermore, underrepresented demographics face additional challenges as they navigate this process.
Our platform differentiates itself by consolidating these opportunities and experiences with a focus on Wellesley and a goal of inclusivity: making opportunities accessible and providing personalized information regarding opportunities for students of all backgrounds.

A platform for the Wellesley College community and by the Wellesley College community.
